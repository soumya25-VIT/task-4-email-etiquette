5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student:
Do’s
•	The student should think before posting anything online 
•	The student should use strong passwords to keep accounts secure 
•	The student should verify information before sharing it 
•	The student should communicate respectfully with others 
•	The student should manage time to balance social media and studies

Don’ts
•	The student should not share personal or sensitive information publicly 
•	The student should avoid posting offensive or harmful content 
•	The student should not accept requests from unknown individuals 
•	The student should avoid clicking on suspicious links 
•	The student should not engage in online arguments or negative interactions
